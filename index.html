Meddenovo Drug Design

AI-powered, Nature inspired, Cyclic Peptide Design

Who we are

Meddenovo Drug Design is a biotech company based in Lyon, France, developing advanced computational technologies for peptide drug discovery. We work at the intersection of artificial intelligence, physics-based modeling, and molecular design to support the discovery of next-generation peptide therapeutics in collaboration with industrial partners.

What we do

We develop Mexa, an AI-powered computational platform specialized in the de novo design of cyclic peptides. Mexa explores tens of millions of peptide candidates per target, combining large-scale AI-driven generation with physics-based evaluation and refinement. The platform can initiate discovery without requiring experimental input, enabling rapid hit identification across internal and partnered drug discovery programs.

Our value proposition 

Meddenovo designs viable cyclic peptide drug candidates when no lead molecule exists. By uniting broad chemical space exploration with rigorous biophysical validation, Mexa delivers high-quality, target-specific hits within short timelines. This approach reduces early discovery risk and opens access to peptide-based modalities for challenging targets, particularly in oncology, radiopharmaceuticals, and metabolic diseases.
Cyclic peptides occupy a unique space between small molecules and biologics. Their structural rigidity enables strong and selective target binding, while their size and versatility allow them to access challenging biological interfaces beyond the reach of traditional drugs. This combination positions cyclic peptides as a high-potential modality for the next generation of therapeutics.
